**💳 Payment Transaction Monitoring Dashboard**

📌 **Project Overview**
This project focuses on analyzing transaction data from ATM, POS, and E-commerce channels to monitor performance, identify failure patterns, and generate business insights.

🎯 **Objectives**
Monitor transaction volume and revenue
Analyze approval and failure rates
Identify top decline reasons
Track performance across channels and merchants

🛠️ **Tools Used**
SQL (Data Analysis)
Excel (Data Cleaning & Pivot Analysis)
Power BI (Dashboard & Visualization)

📊 **Key Metrics**
Total Transactions
Success Rate & Failure Rate
Total Revenue
Channel-wise Performance
Top Merchants

🔍 **Key Insights**
ATM channel contributes the highest transaction volume
Overall success rate is ~70%, indicating a 30% failure rate requiring investigation
Decline rates are evenly distributed across channels, suggesting issuer-side issues
Top decline reasons include insufficient funds and authorization failures
